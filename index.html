Getting Around Nice

Electric Scooter

Feel free to use the scooter to get into town quickly (one person only).

The anti-theft lock keys are on the apartment's key ring : always lock it in the city.

Helmet is mandatory : you'll find a pink, adjustable one at the entrance.

⚠ Be careful on the road, it can be dangerous !

Tram & Bus

Nearest tram stop : "Libération" (12 min on foot).

A transport card is available for you. Top it up with the "Lignes d'Azur Mobile" app or at ticket machines.

Travelling as two ? Validate the card twice each ride.
